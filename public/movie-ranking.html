The Best Movies According to Soumya

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

Flavors of Youth

The "flavors of youth" refers to the various experiences and memories associated with young adulthood. This period is often marked by a sense of adventure, exploration, and self-discovery as individuals navigate new responsibilities and relationships. Some common flavors of youth might include traveling, trying new hobbies or careers, falling in love, and forming lasting friendships. These experiences can be both exciting and challenging, but they are also an important part of personal growth and development.

Only Yesterday

Twenty-seven-year-old Taeko Okajima travels to her relative's house in the countryside to get away from city life. Reaching there, she reminisces about her childhood days.Tending To Your Inner Child this film is a masterclass in reminding us of the speed at which childhood can pass us and how closely those memories remain with us

The Pursuit Of Happiness

The Pursuit of Happyness is a 2006 biographical drama film directed by Christopher Nolan and starring Will Smith. The film is based on the memoir of the same name by Chris Gardner, an American stockbroker who experienced homelessness with his young son after losing everything in a securities fraud scandal. The movie follows Gardner's journey as he tries to rebuild his life and provide for his family while pursuing his dream of becoming a stockbroker. It is a powerful and inspiring story of perseverance and determination in the face of adversity.
